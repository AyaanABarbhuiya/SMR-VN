Stars Might Remember is a short, online visual novel developed and written by the Intel Tinkerer with the assets produced by -tbd-. Follow the protagonist, a 21-year-old detective who has returned to his childdhood village Hoshigiri, the vilage of the stars, hoping to reunite with his lost friend Yui. Instead of nostalgia, he is faced by a string of mysterious murders linked to a old belief. Why is this happening? Who is responsible? Play on to find out!
